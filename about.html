Our Beginning

Nourishing Your
Everyday Life

At Appleberry Stories, we bring you wholesome grains and seeds, carefully sourced to support your family's health and wellbeing. Our journey began with a simple belief: food should be pure, honest, and full of life.

Founded in 2022 in Sale, Manchester, we started as a small family project with big dreams of making premium organic seeds accessible to every UK household — without compromise on quality, ethics, or taste.

  • ✓ Sourced from certified organic farms across the globe
  • ✓ Rigorously tested for purity and freshness before every batch
  • ✓ Packaged sustainably with minimal environmental impact
  • ✓ Gluten-free, additive-free, and naturally nourishing

Our Mission

Natural Grains,
Wholesome Moments

We believe that what you eat shapes how you feel. That's why every decision we make — from farmer partnerships to packaging design — is guided by our commitment to purity, sustainability, and your family's wellbeing.

Our name, "Appleberry Stories," comes from the small moments of nourishment that build big, healthy lives. Every bowl of quinoa, every sprinkle of chia seeds — these are your stories of choosing better.
