🌾 Products
Are your grains and seeds organic? +
Yes, absolutely! All our grains and seeds are certified organic and responsibly sourced. We hold Soil Association certification, which means every product meets rigorous organic standards — from the soil it grows in to the packaging it arrives in.
Are your products gluten-free? +
All our grains and seeds are naturally gluten-free and safe for sensitive diets, including those with coeliac disease. However, we recommend checking individual product labels as we cannot guarantee a completely gluten-free production environment.
Where do your products come from? +
Our seeds and grains are sourced from certified organic farms around the world — including Bolivia for quinoa, Canada for flaxseeds, and Mexico for chia seeds. We maintain direct relationships with our farming partners and regularly audit their practices.

📦 Storage
How should I store my seeds? +
Keep seeds in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to maintain freshness and flavour. Once opened, reseal the packet tightly or transfer to an airtight container. Most seeds last 6–12 months when stored correctly.
Can I freeze seeds and grains? +
Yes! Freezing is a great way to extend the shelf life of seeds and grains, particularly flaxseeds which are high in oils. Store in an airtight container in the freezer for up to 12 months. Thaw at room temperature before use.

💚 Health
How much should I eat per day? +
We recommend 1–2 tablespoons (10–20g) of seeds per day as part of a balanced diet. Seeds are nutrient-dense, so a little goes a long way! Sprinkle on porridge, salads, yoghurt or blend into smoothies for easy daily intake.
Are your products suitable for children? +
Our seeds and grains are suitable for most people including children, though we recommend consulting a healthcare provider for very young children (under 2 years). For older children, seeds can be ground or blended for easier digestion and nutrient absorption.
